How Points System Works


Each Vote = 1 Point

Each Comment = 2 Points

Each Like on Each Comment = 1 Point

The total points define the winner in any war.


If you want your favourite to win:

  • * Vote for your favorite side
  • * Comment to justify your Vote
  • * Like other good comments for your favorite side
  • * Share the war on Facebook / Twitter / WhatsApp to get more votes for your favorite side

About Versus War

Versuswar is as the name suggest a war between two entities. There are several rivalries which have lasted many centuries without a definitive outcome. This website puts the rivals against one another and lets the fans decide. Who is better, who should win, who loses? This website is a platform for arguments. The stronger support an argument has the higher their chances of winning. People decide who wins. Votes name the victor. This is a platform for people to justify their support & to be the voice of the voiceless. Here the arguments, voices, and votes of the people hold the power.

This power doubles up when the voices can inspire. Each opinion, comment, or justification which inspires others to vote or which can motivate others to support the argument is twice as powerful as one vote. Browse the website to see the fights from a variety of categories. Make yourself heard & have your opinion matter by presenting an argument, voting for a side or supporting an argument. You have to power push someone or something to victory or let them fall in defeat. If the things you are most passionate about are not on the website, then make a suggestion to bring them on the website. We will make sure that you too can stage your own fights & invite people to have their voices heard on versuswar.com
